officium-rs

A pure-Rust port of the Divinum Officium liturgical engine. Resolves the day's office and renders the full Tridentine Mass under any of five Roman rubrics — 1570, 1910, 1939, 1955, 1960 — at 100% (R60: 99.7%) parity with the upstream Perl reference.
